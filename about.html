About this app

TOEFL Academic is a photo-based English learning app. Each lesson includes three levels (Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced), vocabulary with Persian translation, and TOEFL-style practice.

How to use

  • Open a lesson from Lessons.
  • Pick a Practice level and follow the tasks.
  • Use the bilingual vocabulary (EN/FA) to study and review.
  • Install as a PWA for faster access and offline usage.

Offline

After you open lessons once, the app caches files for offline use. If you update the app and things look old, use Settings → Clear cache.
